---
title: Calendar Permissions
description: Setting up calendar permissions for Visitd
---

<Info>
Visitd needs your permission to add events to your calendar. Visitd requires full access to your calendar in order to choose which calendar to save visits to.
</Info>

On the next screen, click on Enable Save To Calendar.
<div style="width: 33.333333%; margin: 1.25rem auto;">
<img src="/onboarding/enableCalendar.png" width="200" height="400" />
</div>

On the popup, click on Allow Full Access.

<div style="width: 33.333333%; margin: 1.25rem auto;">
<img src="/onboarding/calendarAccess.png" width="200" height="400" />
</div>

Once both Location Tracking the Calendar Access is approved, Visitd is ready to go.
