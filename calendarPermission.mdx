__Calendar Permission__

Clicking on the Calendar Access item will launch the System Preferences application and open the settings for Visitd. 
<div style="width: 33.333333%; margin: 1.25rem auto;">
<img src="/home/settings.png" width="200" height="400" />
</div>

Clicking on Calendars will allow you to set the level of access Visitd has to your calendars. Calendar access should be 
__Full Access__.

<img src="/home/settinsCalendars.png" width="200" height="400" />
</div>
